What We Do
Our Electrical Services

From minor repairs to major installations, our licensed electricians handle it all — for homes and businesses across Evansville and Southwest Indiana.

Electrical Installations

Light switches, outlets, ceiling fans, smoke detectors, circuit breakers, generators, EV chargers, recessed lighting & more.

Lighting Installations

Indoor & outdoor lighting, recessed, track, chandeliers, LED, motion-activated, dusk-to-dawn, security lights, smart home controls.

Electrical Repairs

Switches, outlets, circuit breakers, underground wiring, code violations, safety inspections, and troubleshooting of all types.

Appliance Hook-Ups

Dishwashers, stoves, dryers, hot tubs, generators, EV chargers, home theaters. Dedicated circuits & 110V to 220V conversions.

Panel Upgrades

Outdated or overloaded panels replaced with modern, code-compliant service panels. 100A to 200A upgrades and full rewiring.

24/7 Emergency Service

Power outages, burning smells, sparking outlets — electrical emergencies can't wait. We're on call around the clock.
