Privacy Policy

Your privacy and safety matter to us.

Last updated: June 2026

At Evansville Electrician Guys, it is our desire for you to have a positive and safe experience while you are browsing our website. We want you to know that we do not store any "cookies" or share or sell your private information to anyone outside of the electrical industry.

If you have any questions concerning our privacy policy, please send us an email or call during normal business hours. We look forward to serving your electrical needs soon.

Information We Collect

When you submit a contact form on our website, we collect the information you provide (such as your name, phone number, email address, and description of the work you need). This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and provide you with an estimate or to schedule service.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit to contact you regarding your electrical service request. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your information is only shared within the context of providing you with our electrical services.

Cookies

Our website does not store cookies on your device. We do not use tracking cookies or any third-party advertising cookies.

Third-Party Services

Our contact form is powered by Formspree, a third-party form processing service. When you submit a form, your data is processed by Formspree in order to deliver your message to us. Please review Formspree's privacy policy for details on how they handle form submissions.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to contact us by phone if you have concerns about transmitting sensitive information online.
